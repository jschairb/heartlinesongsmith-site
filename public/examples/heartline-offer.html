Frequently Asked Questions

How can you create a song so fast?

Our secret is a "human-in-the-loop" system. We use AI to accelerate the administrative and drafting steps—like creating your Track Vision—which allows our human artists to focus exclusively on what they do best: making incredible music.

What is the "Premier Vid"?

It's a personalized music video experience! Our automated system creates a video that feels like an MTV-style world premiere, featuring a VJ host that matches your song's genre, a peek behind the creative curtain, and a full lyric video so you can sing along.

Do I get to give feedback?

Yes! The "Track Vision" document is your chance to make sure we've captured the lyrical direction and overall vibe perfectly. Once you approve it, our team gets to work on the final production.
